Tactical Brief

The second fastest unit in the ISDF's standard armament, the Scout features dual mini-guns and a FAF rocket as its standard loadout. A special hardpoint is available but remains empty by default.

Because of their great speed and excellent radar range, Scouts make ideal command vehicles. Scouts automatically mark enemy units whenever they come into radar range, providing an excellent warning and identification system.

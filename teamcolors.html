LocalPrefs.ini

TeamColorTeamA = "220 20 60 255"
TeamColorTeamB = "0 64 128 255"

How to apply

  1. Find your LocalPrefs.ini at:
    …\steamapps\common\BZ2R\bz2r_res\config\LocalPrefs.ini
  2. Copy it to your addon config folder:
    Documents\My Games\Battlezone Combat Commander\
    Create an addon subfolder if it doesn't exist, then place the file inside it.
  3. Paste the two lines above into LocalPrefs.ini, replacing any existing TeamColorTeam entries.
  4. Launch the game — your team colors will be active.
